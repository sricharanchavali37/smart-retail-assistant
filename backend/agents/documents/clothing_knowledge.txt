Clothing Category — Product Knowledge Base

OVERVIEW
The Clothing category covers apparel, fashion accessories, and seasonal wear.
Price range: Rs 25 to Rs 500 per unit.
Average transaction value: Rs 428 per day — most consistent performer across the year.
Most stable category: lowest anomaly count (only 6 anomalous days in 2023).

TOP PRODUCTS
- Casual wear and t-shirts (Rs 25–50): High volume, budget segment
- Formal shirts and trousers (Rs 300–500): Premium segment, lower frequency
- Seasonal clothing — jackets, kurtas (Rs 50–300): Strong seasonal spikes
- Accessories — belts, scarves (Rs 25–50): Impulse purchases, high attach rate

DEMAND PATTERNS
- Steady demand year-round with mild seasonal variation
- Peak months: October, November, December (festive and wedding season)
- Weekend sales slightly higher than weekday (10% average premium)
- Average customer age: 36 years
- Gender split: roughly equal male and female customers

ANOMALY INSIGHTS
- Only 6 anomalous days detected in 2023 — most predictable category
- Anomalies are usually tied to flash sales or festival promotions
- Rolling 7-day mean is the most reliable forecast baseline for Clothing

RESTOCKING GUIDANCE
- Clothing has longer shelf life — maintain 21-day safety stock
- Size distribution: stock S:M:L:XL in ratio 1:3:3:2 for adult apparel
- Seasonal items: order 8 weeks in advance for festival season
- Lead time: 3–7 business days for domestic suppliers

RETURN POLICY
- Clothing can be returned within 30 days if tags are intact and unworn
- Sale items: exchange only, no cash refund
- Washed or altered items cannot be returned
- Size exchanges are free within 15 days of purchase
