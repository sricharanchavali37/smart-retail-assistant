Beauty Category — Product Knowledge Base

OVERVIEW
The Beauty category covers skincare, cosmetics, personal care, and wellness products.
Price range: Rs 25 to Rs 500 per unit.
Average transaction value: Rs 390 per day across all beauty purchases.

TOP PRODUCTS
- Moisturisers and face serums (Rs 300–500): Premium segment, high margin
- Lipsticks and makeup kits (Rs 50–100): High volume, frequent repurchase
- Sunscreens and SPF products (Rs 25–50): Budget segment, consistent demand
- Hair care products (Rs 25–300): Broad range, seasonal demand

DEMAND PATTERNS
- Peak months: February (Valentine's Day), October and November (festive season), December
- Weekend sales are 15% higher than weekday sales on average
- Female customers account for roughly 60% of Beauty purchases
- Average customer age: 35 years

ANOMALY INSIGHTS
- 17 anomalous days detected in 2023 (highest single-day revenue: Rs 3,690)
- Anomalies cluster in May and December — driven by promotional events
- Z-score above 3.0 indicates a genuine demand spike worth investigating

RESTOCKING GUIDANCE
- Maintain 14-day safety stock for top 5 SKUs
- Reorder point: when rolling 7-day average drops below 60% of monthly mean
- Lead time from supplier: 3–5 business days

RETURN POLICY
- Beauty products are non-returnable once opened or seal is broken
- Unopened products can be returned within 30 days with original receipt
- Damaged products qualify for full replacement within 7 days of purchase
