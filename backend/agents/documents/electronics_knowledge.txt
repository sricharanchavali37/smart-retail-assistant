Electronics Category — Product Knowledge Base

OVERVIEW
The Electronics category covers gadgets, accessories, consumer electronics, and tech products.
Price range: Rs 25 to Rs 500 per unit.
Average transaction value: Rs 421 per day — highest average price point of all categories.
Most volatile category: largest revenue swings and highest anomaly count.

TOP PRODUCTS
- Smartphones and accessories (Rs 300–500): High value, low volume
- Earphones and audio devices (Rs 50–300): Mid segment, steady demand
- Charging cables and adapters (Rs 25–50): High volume, low margin
- Smartwatches and wearables (Rs 300–500): Growing segment

DEMAND PATTERNS
- Peak months: May, June (summer), December (gifting season)
- Electronics had 30 anomalous days in 2023 — highest of all categories
- Demand spikes strongly on weekends and during sales events
- Average customer age: 38 years
- Male customers account for roughly 55% of Electronics purchases

ANOMALY INSIGHTS
- Highest single-day revenue recorded: Rs 5,200 (June 24, 2023)
- Anomaly z-score threshold: 4.0 for Electronics (higher baseline variance)
- May 2023 had 5 anomalous days — likely a flash sale event
- December anomalies driven by holiday gifting

RESTOCKING GUIDANCE
- Electronics inventory is high-value — avoid overstocking
- Maintain 7-day safety stock for fast-moving accessories
- Premium items (Rs 500): order-on-demand model recommended
- Lead time from supplier: 5–10 business days for imported goods

WARRANTY AND RETURN POLICY
- Electronics must be unused and in original sealed packaging for return
- Return window: 7 days from purchase date
- Defective products: replacement within 14 days, no questions asked
- No returns accepted for software-activated or registered devices
