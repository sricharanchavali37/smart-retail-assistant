Smart Retail Store — General Policies and Operations

STORE OVERVIEW
Smart Retail operates across Beauty, Clothing, and Electronics categories.
Total transactions in 2023: 1,000
Total revenue tracked: Rs 418,665 (annual)
Average daily revenue per category: Rs 418

PRICING POLICY
- Price points: Rs 25, Rs 30, Rs 50, Rs 100, Rs 300, Rs 500
- No dynamic pricing — fixed price per SKU
- Seasonal discounts applied store-wide during festival months
- Bulk purchase discount: 10% on orders above 4 units of same SKU

CUSTOMER DEMOGRAPHICS
- Age range: 18 to 64 years
- Average customer age: 35.6 years
- Gender split: approximately 52% female, 48% male
- Top buying age group: 30–45 years

ANOMALY ALERT POLICY
- Anomaly score above 0.55: send alert to store manager
- Anomaly score above 0.65: immediate stock audit triggered
- Revenue z-score above 3.0: flag for pricing error review
- Consecutive anomalies on same category: escalate to regional manager

DEMAND FORECASTING POLICY
- Forecasts generated daily using XGBoost model
- Forecast horizon: up to 14 days ahead
- Model retraining: monthly with latest transaction data
- Acceptable MAPE threshold: under 15% for production use

CUSTOMER SERVICE
- Store hours: 9 AM to 9 PM, all days
- Customer support: in-store and via online chat
- Loyalty programme: 1 point per Rs 50 spent, redeemable after 500 points
- Grievance resolution: within 48 hours for all complaints

DATA PRIVACY
- Customer transaction data retained for 3 years
- No personal data shared with third parties without consent
- All data processed under applicable privacy regulations
